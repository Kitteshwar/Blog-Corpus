NPS registrationNPS registration is the process of enrolling in the National Pension System (NPS), a pension scheme offered by the government of India. The scheme aims to provide citizens with a retirement savings option and regular pension income post-retirement. To register for NPS, individuals must fill out an online application form and submit the necessary documents. Once the registration process is complete, the individual will receive a Permanent Retirement Account Number (PRAN) which is required to make contributions and manage their account.Online methodThe steps for NPS registration online method are:Visit the official website of the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA)Click on the ‘NPS’ tabSelect ‘Registration’Fill in the online registration formUpload the required documentsSubmit the formMake the initial contributionReceive the Permanent Retirement Account Number (PRAN)Once the registration process is completed, the individual will receive an acknowledgement number and PRAN, which will be required for further transactions.Offline methodThe steps for NPS registration offline method are:Obtain the NPS registration form from a Point of Presence (POP) or from the PFRDA website.Fill in the registration form with accurate details.Attach the required documents along with the formSubmit the form and the documents to the nearest Point of Presence (POP)Make the initial contributionReceive the Permanent Retirement Account Number (PRAN)Once the registration process is completed, the individual will receive an acknowledgement number and PRAN, which will be required for further transactions.How to fill the NPS Registration Form?To fill the NPS Registration Form, individuals must:provide their personal details such as name, date of birth, address, contact details, and identification number. provide information about their nominee, occupation, and income. upload the required documents and make the initial contributionensure that all the information provided is accurate and matches the supporting documents. It’s important to read the instructions and guidelines before filling the form to avoid any errors or mistakes.Online filling of NPS Registration Form To fill the NPS Registration Form online, individuals must visit the official website of PFRDA, fill in the online registration form with accurate details, upload the required documents and submit the form. Here is how applicants can fill in each of the details in the NPS registration form:Contact details tab Steps to fill Contact Details Tab in NPS registration form:Enter your addressProvide your mobile numberFill in your email addressBank and other details tab Steps to fill Bank and Other Details Tab in NPS registration form:Enter your bank account numberProvide your bank IFSC codeFill in your MICR codeSelect mode of contributionNomination details tab Steps to fill Nomination Details Tab in NPS registration form:Enter nominee nameProvide nominee’s relationship with youFill in nominee’s date of birthEnter nominee’s addressDocuments upload tab Here are the steps to fill the documents upload tab in an NPS registration form:Gather the required documents, such as proof of identity, proof of address, and proof of date of birth. Make sure they are in electronic format, such as PDF or JPEG.Go to the Documents Upload tab of the NPS registration form.Select the type of document you want to upload, such as proof of identity or proof of address.Click on the “Browse” or “Choose File” button to select the electronic copy of the document from your computer or device.Once the document is selected, click on the “Upload” button to upload it to the form.Repeat steps 3 to 5 for each required document.Make sure that all the documents are uploaded successfully and verify that all the information is correct.Submit the form once all the required documents are uploaded.Photo and signature details tabTo update the photo and signature details tab in an NPS registration form, log in to your account on the official NPS website and navigate to the “Update Profile” section. This includes viewing account balance, transaction history, and making contributions or changes to their investment options.To login to the NSDL NPS portal, go to the website and click on the “Login” button. Enter your Permanent Retirement Account Number (PRAN) and password, and complete the captcha verification. Click on “Submit” to access your account.How to reset the password on NSDL NPS Portal?To reset your password on the NSDL NPS portal, go to the login page and click on “Forgot Password.” Enter your PAN number and registered email address. Follow the prompts to reset your password. Make sure to keep your new password safe and secure.NPS login through KFintech NPS portalKFintech NPS portal is an online platform provided by KFin Technologies Private Limited. Enter your net banking credentials and follow the prompts to complete the login process. Make sure to keep your net banking credentials safe and secure.NPS Helpline NPS helpline is a customer service provided by the National Pension System (NPS) to assist subscribers with queries, issues, and grievances related to the scheme. The helpline can be reached via phone, email, or post. The NPS helpline is available during business hours.To contact the National Pension System (NPS) helpline, you can call the toll-free number 1800-22-0180 or write to the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority at info@pfrda.org.in. You can also visit the NPS Trust website at www.npstrust.org.in for more information.How to download the NPS App?To download the NPS App, go to the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) and search for “NPS App”. Select the official app and tap “Download” or “Install”. Once the download is complete, open the app and follow the instructions to create an account or log in.What are NPS Online Services?NPS Online Services are a set of online tools provided by the National Pension System (NPS) in India, which allow individuals to access and manage their pension accounts and investment options via the internet. These services include account opening, contribution, withdrawal, and more.ConclusionHaving a NPS account can bring in multiple benefits to the subscribers, including, reliable source of income post retirement, offers significant tax benefits, accessible and easy to enrol in, and anyone can join NPS system. The above-mentioned information can allow NPS subscribers to easily access their NPS accounts online.Some Frequently Asked Questions1. How can one check his or her NPS account balance online?The accumulated account balance of the account holder can be checked online on NSDL portal or KFintech website. To avail the facility, login using your id and password along with PRAN.2. What is PRAN and PRAN Kit?The NPS subscriber receives a PRAN card with his or her name, father’s name, photograph, signature and subscriber’s date of birth once the registration is complete. This card is proof of the fact that all information provided is complete on the CRA system. The PRAN kit has a PRAN card, subscriber’s details and an information booklet in it.3. What is IPIN?IPIN is a password used to access your NPS account. 4. How can I reset my IPIN online?One must go to the NPS account login and click on the ‘Forgot Password’ option to select the ‘Instant reset IPIN’. All the details required must be entered in and thereafter, the account holder will receive an OTP on his or her registered phone number or email address. The OTP must be entered in and submitted on the portal to reset your password. 5. Can I avail NPS related services online?Yes, some NPS related services can be availed online. They are – change of personal details (address, mobile number, email address), generating or resetting IPIN, change scheme preferences, viewing account statements online, ePRAN card view and reprint, Aadhaar seeding and changing account details.6. How to request for a duplicate PRAN card?In case one has lost or damaged his or her PRAN card, the S2 form has to be filled by the nearby PoP-SP. Post verification, the POP or POPSP will enter the concern and authorize the said request in the CRA system. A processed, duplicate PRAN card will be sent to the subscriber.7. How can I change NPS account details?Any changes in the account details will be done by the POP-SP. These can include changes about the name, date of birth, update of photograph and signature etc. This must be initiated by submitting a written request to the POP-SP. Additionally, certain forms must be submitted (each concerns a different purpose) – Form UPS2- To change personal or nomination details or request for reissue of T-PIN/I-PIN or Reprint of PRAN card.Form S3- To request for change in Scheme Preference or Switch or change of employment.Form S7 – To request for change in Photograph and/or Signature.Interesting reads..Share the article                      Get exclusive content
