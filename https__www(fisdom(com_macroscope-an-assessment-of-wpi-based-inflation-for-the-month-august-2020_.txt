Mathematically, it can be written as: (Price of basket in current period / Price of basket in base period) x 100 Currently, 2012 is used as base year for inflation calculation and reading purposes.What does WPI Inflation data mean for Financial Markets?Tracking prices at the factory gate before the retail level, WPI helps understand the supply and demand dynamics in industry, manufacturing, and construction. It is also a good parameter to evaluate the investment potential of a country by foreign and domestic investors alike.WPI Inflation Reading – August 2020What is the latest reading?India’s Wholesale inflation up at 0.16% after being in negative zone for 4 months. It was at -0.58% in July and is up from May lows of -3.2%.Investor TakeawayWPI recorded positive figures, signalling that producers are slowly regaining pricing power.
