AkshathaSajumonWritten by - Akshatha SajumonFebruary 20, 2023  7 minutes  EPF Form 19 is required if a member wants to withdraw funds from the account through final settlement. This form can also be used for withdrawal of pension benefits or to avail a non-refundable advance from PF account. The option of withdrawing funds through this form can be used while leaving employment due to retirement or other reason. However, the option of availing an advance from PF account is only available during a member’s service term if some of the conditions are met.CategoryPF Form 19Used forFinal settlementEligibilityExisting employees who wish to close their existing EPF accountMandatoryFor final settlementModeOnline and offline form fillingTimelineTo be filled at least 2 months post leaving the job  Table of Contents  hide   1 Contents of Form 19   2 Important factors to note while filling Form 19   3 Steps to fill EPF Form 19 online   4 Steps to fill EPF Form 19 offline   5 What is a Composite Claim Form?   6 How to check the EPF settlement claim status?   7 End Note   8 FAQs Contents of Form 19Form 19 comprises the following details:Member nameFather’s or Husband’s Name (applicable in case of married women)Date of BirthName and Address of the employing companyP.F. Account No.UANPermanent Account Number (PAN)Date of joining companyDate of leaving serviceReason for leaving servicePostal address of memberMode of paymentImportant factors to note while filling Form 19Before filing Form 19, EPF members must consider the below-mentioned important aspects:A member must activate his/her UAN on the EPF member portalAs a mandatory requirement, the bank account, PAN and mobile number of the EPF member must be linked with UANIf a member is not eligible for final settlement, he/she will not be able to see Form 19 in the withdrawal form sectionThe member’s and employer’s signature are required while filling the Form 19Form 19 can be used only after completion of two months of leaving the service or start of retirementEPF member must ensure to provide his/her registered mobile number for final settlementPAN is mandatory for claiming final EPF settlementThe employing organisation’s signature and seal are mandatory for offline settlement of EPFDid you know – You can avail EPF services through the UMANG appThe Indian government now allows eligible employees to avail EPF-related services through its Umang app. The app is helpful for both employees and employers. Some of the extra services offered through the app include:Locating establishments and EPFO officesKeeping track of claim statusAccount details using SMSWhat’s more, there is also a live chat option on the app which is provided by the EPFO!Steps to fill EPF Form 19 onlineAfter leaving the job, an EPF member can either choose to settle the PF account or transfer the balance to the new EPF account with the new company. Those who want to make a final settlement must fill Form 19.  Here is how an EPF member can fill Form 19 online:On the EPF member portal, a member must login to his/her UAN accountWithin the “Online Services” section, select option “Claim (Form – 31, 19 & 10C)” A user must then enter the last 4 digits of the linked bank account and select “Verify”An employee must provide his/her registered mobile number at the start of the formSelect “Yes” to proceed with signing the “Certificate of Undertaking”From the drop-down menu under the “I want to apply for” section, select the option “Only PF Withdrawal (Form-19)” Once the new form section expands, the user must mention his/her complete address. After selecting the disclaimer, click on “Get Aadhaar OTP”User will then receive an OTP on his/her registered mobile numberUser must then enter this OTP in the field provided before submitting the applicationA reference number is generated post successful submission of the applicationThe amount withdrawn will be deposited within 15-20 days in the bank account linked to user’s UANIf an EPF member requires the final settlement payment through cheque, he/she must fill the advance stamp receipt. This means a revenue stamp of Re. 1 has to be affixed on the form and the member should sign across the stamp.  Those who choose the ECS option (electronic credit), do not need to fill an advance stamp.Steps to fill EPF Form 19 offlineThe steps to fill the EPF Form 19 offline are as follows:Obtain a copy of the EPF Form 19 from the nearest EPFO office or download it from the official website.Fill in the required details in the form, such as personal details, employer details, and PF account details.Ensure that the form is signed by both the employee and the employer.Attach a cancelled cheque or a copy of the passbook to the form to facilitate the transfer of funds.Submit the filled-in form along with the necessary documents to the EPFO office.What is a Composite Claim Form?For EPF withdrawal, EPFO requires the applicant to fill one form, which is known as the Composite Claim Form (CCF). This form can be used to make partial or full withdrawal of PF funds, It can also be used to withdraw  money lying in one’s EPS account. Depending on whether an applicant has the Aadhaar number or not, there are two forms –a. Composite Claim Form ( Aadhaar) andb. Composite Claim Form (Non- Aadhaar)How to check the EPF settlement claim status?To check the EPF Settlement Claim Status, an applicant needs the claim ID which is available on the official EPF website. Here are the steps to be followed for checking settlement claim status once the user has the claim ID:Visit the EPFO portal and enter the claim ID in the search bar.The current status of the claim and other related details will be seen hereIf the claim is approved, user will receive the settlement amount in his/her bank account within the given timeline.End NoteEmployees’ Provident Fund (EPF) is a retirement pension scheme in which an employee and his/her employer are required to contribute a portion of the monthly salary towards the EPF account. This is applicable during the service period, after which, the EPF member can withdraw the lump-sum amount for retirement purpose. FAQsWhy is EPF Form 19 important?By using EPF Form 19 for online claims processing, an EPF member does not require his/her employer’s attestation. Such claims can also be processed within 5 days post online application.Are there any pre-conditions to PF withdrawal through Form 19?PF withdrawal through Form 19 can be done only after the employer updates the exit date or date of end of service of the EPF member. In absence of this, the EPF department will halt the withdrawal process.Is it necessary to withdraw the pension amount along with the PF amount?No, it is not necessary to withdraw pension along with PF amount.What if an employer does not update the exit date of an employee at the time of PF withdrawal?If an employer does not update an employee’s exit date for PF withdrawal despite reminders, the employee can register a complaint through the EPFO portal. If a written complaint is filed with the EPF commissioner, subsequent action is taken for issue resolution.Which form can an employee use for offline application to withdraw PF amount?Composite Claims Form /9 CCF is to be used for offline application to withdraw PF amount.EPF Advance Form for Partial withdrawal or Advance from EPF(Form 31)EPF – All You Need to Know About EPFEPF Withdrawal: How to Fill PF Form & Get Claim OnlineHow much can you withdraw from an EPF account due to COVID-19?Form 15G – How to Fill Form 15G for PF Withdrawal?Share the article                      Get exclusive content
