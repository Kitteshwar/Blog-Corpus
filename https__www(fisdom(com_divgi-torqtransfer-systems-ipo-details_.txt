The company is engaged in the business of developing system-level transfer cases, torque couplers, and dual-clutch automatic transmission solutions. The company has big domestic names like Tata Motors and Mahindra & Mahindra as its customers and also has a global footprint in countries like UK and USA in the west to China, Thailand, Korea, and Japan in the east. They are also the sole Indian manufacturer and exporter of transfer cases to global OEMs and the only manufacturer of torque couplers in the country. The company is a manufacturer and supplier of a wide range of products that includes, Torque transfer systems (which includes four-wheel-drive (4WD) and all-wheel-drive (AWD) products)Synchronizer systems for manual transmissions and DCTComponents for the above-mentioned product categories for torque transfer systems and synchronizer systems in manual transmission, DCT, and EVs.Financials of the companyDivgi TorqTransfer Systems Limited financials are given in the table below:Year Total Assets (Rs.
