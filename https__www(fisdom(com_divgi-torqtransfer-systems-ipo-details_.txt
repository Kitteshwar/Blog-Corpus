The company is engaged in the business of developing system-level transfer cases, torque couplers, and dual-clutch automatic transmission solutions. The company has big domestic names like Tata Motors and Mahindra & Mahindra as its customers and also has a global footprint in countries like UK and USA in the west to China, Thailand, Korea, and Japan in the east.
