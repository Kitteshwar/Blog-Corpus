These belong to companies with market capitalization running in billions, often market leaders, or at least are one of the top three companies in their respective sector. Here, we will learn more about blue-chip funds and stocks, explore various aspects surrounding this investment option.Table of contentsWhat are Blue Chip Stocks in India?Who should invest in Blue Chip Stocks?What are the main features of Blue Chip Stocks?Why should you invest in Blue Chip Stocks?Best Blue Chip Funds and Stocks in IndiaHow to Invest in Blue Chip Stocks and Funds?ConclusionFAQsWhat are Blue Chip Stocks in India?Blue chip stocks can cope better even during adverse market conditions and help investors to generate high returns. These often come for a premium since they belong to dominant companies within the industry. Blue-chip investment may also yield consistent dividends for investors in the long term.Some important points to note about blue chip are:These companies mostly generate stable returns in the long run. Consistent returns in normal course protect investors against inflation, recession, and other market movements.Blue chip companies generally have a stable debt-to-equity ratio.The average return on investment, price to equity ratio, and the interest coverage ratio of such companies contribute to steady performance. These companies usually offer regular dividends to investors, thereby creating a regular source of income for investors.Who should invest in Blue Chip Stocks?Blue chip stocks are considered safe investment options, and they can also help investors in balancing the overall portfolio risk. However, the common assumption while making such an investment is that a company has diverse operations and may not be affected by fluctuations in the stock market. Even if a company’s financial performance is not as per expectations for some period, it can cover most of the losses incurred in a business function from its other business segments.Such stocks are mostly preferred by cash-rich investors who have experience in making high-value investments. Investors who do not want their investments to be affected by any major economic downturn can invest in blue chip stocks. While investors can expect income through dividend payouts from most of these stocks, it is advisable to ensure sufficient diversification of a portfolio to avoid any company-specific risks. Also, since most of the blue chip investment offer better returns in the long term, investors who are comfortable remaining invested for a 7-plus years time span must consider blue chip stocks.What are the main features of Blue Chip Stocks?Some of the key features of blue chip stocks are as listed below:Companies with an excellent financial track recordA strong balance sheetConsistent growth rateStrong managerial teamWhy invest in Blue Chip Stocks?Some of the key traits that attract investors towards blue-chip stocks are as mentioned below: Superior returns: Since blue chip are industry leaders, they have higher chances of growth and therefore can deliver higher returns.Low Volatility: Blue Chip stocks are of companies that are financially sound with competent management & strong corporate governance practices. This makes the companies stable and less affected by market volatilities.Regular Income Through Dividends: Blue Chip investment belong to companies that have huge cash surplus and can offer regular dividends to the investors.Best Blue Chip Funds and Stocks in IndiaBased on market capitalization, there are many blue chip companies in India whose stocks generate good to high returns in the long term. Investors who are looking to grow their wealth in the long term without having exposure to a lot of risk and market volatility must consider investing in blue chip stocks.What are blue chip stocks?A blue-chip stock belongs to a large corporation that has an excellent reputation. These companies are well-established and financially sound and have operated for many years. Blue chip stocks are also called large-cap stocks and often provide regular dividends to investors.Which Blue Chip Fund Should I invest in?Some of the top performing blue chip funds can be found on the Fisdom  app. Investors must always look at the historic performance of the fund before selecting one.What is the difference between blue chip and common stock?Blue-chip companies are ones that have stood the test of time and have proven themselves through solid performance, even in volatile markets.
