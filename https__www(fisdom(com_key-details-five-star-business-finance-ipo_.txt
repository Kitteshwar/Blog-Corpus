Five Star Business Finance Limited is an NBFC (Non Banking Finance Company) and is in the business of providing secured business loans and small mortgage loans to small and micro-entrepreneurs as well as self-employed individuals. The company also sanctions these loans for home improvement purposes to eligible borrowers. The company has its headquarters in Chennai and has approximately 85% of its network in The southern region of the country. The network of Five Star Business Finance Limited includes 268 branches as of 30th September 2021 that are spread over 126 districts, 8 states, and 1 union territory. The southern states of Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, and Telangana are the key states for this company. The average loan offered by Fiver Star Business is in the range of Rs. 1,00,000 to Rs. 10,00,000 with the tenure for the same being approximately 24 months to 84 months. The loans are disbursed on the basis of the company’s internal evaluation model that ascertains the future cashflows as well as secures the loan against collateral. This secures the asset quality of the company even in the cases of demonetization, GST changes, Covid, etc.   Financials of the companyThe core financials of the company are tabled below.Year Total Assets (Rs.
