The company manufactures, distributes, and markets different products in the agrochemical sector like insecticides, fungicides, herbicides, plant growth regulators, micro fertilizers, and antibiotics. The distribution and marketing of these products are done through licensed brands that are owned by the company as well as generic brands for Indian farmers.
