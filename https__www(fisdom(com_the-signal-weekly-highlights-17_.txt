The rules apply to companies without a track record, and these shareholders can, post April 2022, sell only 50% of their holding on the day the company lists post an Initial Public Offering (IPO). It touched a lifetime high of USD 642.453 billion in the week ended September 3, 2021.During the reporting week ended December 17, the dip in the forex kitty was on account of a decline in foreign currency assets (FCAs), a major component of the overall reserves.
