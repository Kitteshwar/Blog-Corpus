Hence the UPI method of payments is more preferred by investors.What are the Services Offered by UPI Apps?The Unified Payment Interface or UPI as it is popularly called is an initiative that encourages cashless payments. UPI is a platform which brings various banking services together which can be availed by the users. Due to the ease of payment and quicker instant transactions, UPI method of payment has become very popular among the users. This has also paved the way for many new UPI payment applications which have launched in the past few years.These UPI applications are fairly easy to use and allow you to use the UPI payment method once you have registered on them. Along with that, there are many more services that are offered by the UPI applications which are as follows:1. Money transactionsThe UPI applications allow you to both send money as well as request money. You can send money through the UPI applications by the Virtual Payment Address or VPA which can be a mobile number, bank account and IFSC code or QR scan code. The other important things you need are your 6 digit UPI PIN and the 4 digit password. By using these, you can easily make a payment through the UPI application.You can also request money through the UPI application if you know the VPA of the payer. All you need to do is enter the amount and VPA of the payer to request money from them using the UPI application.2. Transaction HistoryThe UPI applications store your transaction history which you can view and verify using the UPI applications. This helps you to keep a track of your transactions and also guarantee safe transactions.3. Checking Account BalanceOnce you link the UPI application with your registered bank account, you can easily check the account balance through the UPI applications by simply entering the 6 digit UPI PIN. This makes the account balance enquiry process a lot quicker.4. Adding more than one bank accountUPI applications allow you to add more than one bank account. The only condition for doing so is to have all these bank accounts linked to the same mobile number. This allows you to choose from more than one bank account while making payments through the UPI application.5. Setting and updating the UPI PINThe UPI PIN is very important as it is the 6 digit number which you would have to enter every time you want to make a transaction through your registered bank account. When you first register on the UPI application, the application allows you to set the 6 digit UPI PIN of your choice. This PIN has to be confidential and you should be well aware not to share it with anyone. The added advantage of setting your own PIN is that you can update the UPI PIN anytime you want.6. Transaction NotificationsWhenever you make a transaction using the UPI application, these applications send you a notification which reflects your transaction. In case you are not the one who has made the transaction, you can take action immediately. This is an added benefit that the UPI applications offer.7. Managing your Bank accountAs the UPI applications allow you to add more than one bank account, you can access and manage all your bank accounts at one place. This will save you time and you won’t have to open various applications for managing different bank accounts.Conclusion So to conclude, UPI applications offer a number of services at one place which can prove very beneficial to the users that have registered with UPI. These services have made the UPI method one of the most popular methods of payments among users.
