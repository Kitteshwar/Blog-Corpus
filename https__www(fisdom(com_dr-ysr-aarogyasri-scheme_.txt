To combat this, Andhra Pradesh State Government has Launched Dr YSR Aarogyasri Scheme, whose inauguration was done by CM YS Jagan Mohan Reddy. This is the second welfare scheme that the government launched in 2007. Aarogyasri Health Care Trust administers this scheme. It provides financial assistance to economically weaker sections of the population for treatment of serious illnesses, such as cancer, heart disease, and kidney failure. The scheme covers both inpatient and outpatient treatment at network hospitals, and also covers certain procedures such as organ transplants. The scheme is implemented by the government of Andhra Pradesh in partnership with private hospitals.COVID-19 Coverage in YSR Aarogyasri Health SchemeCovid-19 was considered highly contagious during its peak in 2020 and 2021. Under the Aarogyasri health scheme in India, eligible patients can receive cashless healthcare benefits for certain treatments.In-patient careIn-patient care in Aarogyasri health scheme in India refers to medical treatment given to a patient who is required to stay overnight in a hospital. Eligible patients can receive cashless inpatient care for certain treatments.Out-patient treatmentThere are network hospitals and health camps where one can avail the services for the treatment completely cashless. Pre-existing disease coverSuppose the scheme member has the disease before applying for the scheme, then that disease is also covered under the plan. This benefit is not available in other health insurance schemes which are commercial.Follow-up treatment coverTreatments that need follow-up after hospitalization and need money are also covered under this scheme. Family floater coverThis scheme provides benefits to the whole family, known as family-floater Health Insurance Cover: One does not need to make different plans for each family member. People who have applied for this scheme get a financial cover of Rs. 5 Lakh per year per family. Those who need medical help can apply for this aid if they are part of the registered family. Treatments covered in YSR Aarogyasri schemeDr YSR Aarogyasri scheme.covers treatment from simple to critical. below:Families native to Andhra Pradesh and Telangana can apply for this scheme. Those whose family income is below Rs. 5 Lakh can apply.It is a must that they should possess white ration cards. Members of Antodaya Anna Yojana can apply for the scheme.Those who are applying should have 35 acres of dry and wet land. People under this scheme should pay municipal property tax for less The hospital verifies the documents and processes the claim. The scheme also has an easy claim process for the hospitals that are empaneled under the scheme.
